  • What does Ikigai Mean ?
    1. Ikigai lit. means "a reason to get up"

    2. Ikigai refers to defining your personal meaning of life in relation to your talents, passions, and profession, as well as what you can give to the wider world



  • what are the 4 concepts of Ikigai
    1. Work you are good at.

    2. The work that you love.

    3. The work that the world needs.

    4. The work that creates wealth.

    5. With these four elements in place, you can find your Ikigai


  • What are the health benefits of ikigai?
    1. In Japan, ikigai is an essential factor contributing to a person's well-being.

    2. Studies consistently demonstrate that ikigai can improve health, increase longevity, and reduce the risk of disability in old age

    3. Studies show that people who live with a greater sense of purpose tend to engage in more health-promoting activities.
