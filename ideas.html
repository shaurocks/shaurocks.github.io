2. Car reverse sensor/fog sensor

*Materials required written in learning page.

Whenever we park our car someone has to getout of the car and has to see how much distance is behind. But our project solves this problem.
In this car sensor you can adjust the distince of its sensing and also connect many other external devices like bulb, motor etc.
It can also be used as fog sensor. During the time of fog many car acidents occour. Set of two sensors can be used to prevent these acidents.
