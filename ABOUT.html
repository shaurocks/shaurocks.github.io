This page will tell you why you should get vaccinated against Covid-19.



Even though there are less cases of Covid-19 it is not over yet, there are still many cases of Covid-19 coming everyday. It is beacuse still many people are not vaccinated. Many people believe that it is not good to be vaccinated because of some rumors that went viral at the time of lockdown.

Given below are some reasons why should we get vaccinated :-

1) COVID-19 vaccines can help prevent you from getting COVID-19 .

2) It protects your family, friends and teachers .

3) It sets an example for others in your community .

4) COVID-19 vaccines are safe .

5) The vaccines cannot cause COVID-19 because they do not contain the coronavirus .

6) The vaccines do not interact with DNA .

7) Most side effects are caused by the desired immune response .

8) Your risk of COVID-19 infection is reduced by 5 times by being fully vaccinated .

9) Your risk of experiencing severe COVID-19, requiring hospitalization is reduced by 10 times by being fully vaccinated .

10) Your risk of death from COVID-19 is reduced by 10 times by being fully vaccinated .







Vaccines go through various phases of development and testing. There are usually three phases to clinical trials, with the last one designed to assess the ability of the product to protect against disease, which is called efficacy. All phases assess safety.
