What is G20 Summit ?

The G20, or Group of Twenty, is a forum consisting of 19 countries and the European Union.
It was established in 1999 to facilitate discussions and cooperation among major advanced and emerging economies.
The G20 members meet annually at the G20 Summit to address economic and financial issues




About G20 Summit 2023

The 2023 G20 New Delhi summit is the upcoming eighteenth meeting of Group of Twenty,
a summit scheduled to take place in International Exhibition-Convention Centre, Pragati Maidan,
New Delhi in 2023. It will be the first ever G20 summit to be held in India as well as in South Asia.
